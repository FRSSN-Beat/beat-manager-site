Privacy Policy

This policy explains how BEAT Manager handles information.

HomeTerms of Service

Information processed

  • basic profile information supplied by a connected platform;
  • public video identifiers, titles, descriptions and thumbnails;
  • public statistics such as views, likes, comments and shares;
  • local project information entered by the user.

TikTok permissions

The application may request user.info.basic and video.list to display the connected user's basic profile and public videos.

Storage

BEAT Manager is primarily a desktop application. Its catalogue, settings, access tokens and cached thumbnails may be stored locally on the user's computer.

Sharing and sale of data

BEAT Manager does not sell personal information. Information is not shared with third parties except when required to communicate with services the user explicitly connects.

Data control and deletion

Users can disconnect a platform account, remove locally stored credentials and revoke access from the connected platform's account settings.

Contact

Questions can be submitted through the BEAT Manager GitHub repository.
